Genatomy
Genatomy is a visualizing tool for biological data, such as gene expression, genotypes, growth curves, copy number variation and more. It can be used to analyze the data mathematically and to study the biological aspects of the data and the results.

To check what java version is installed on your computer, and for upgrading instructions, read this.

Tutorial for Genatomy is available here, and an extended user manual is available here.

Please feel free to contact us with any question or suggestion at genatomy@gmail.com.

If you use Genatomy for your publication, please cite Litvin et al. PNAS 2009.

Genaomty is freely distributed for academic use but registration is required.
